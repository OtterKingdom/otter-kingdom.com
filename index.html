Features

A love letter from former AAA industry veterans inspired by older tiltes such as The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker.

Online

Participate in an epic co-op adventure with other players or challenge other players in PvP arenas to earn rewards and show off your skills.

Player driven

The world is owned and driven by players with NFTs that actually affect the world on a large scale. How about being a weather god or a time god able to alter the entire world?

Play to Earn

Earn items and win prizes by participating in game events such as PvP tournaments, PvE dungeon rushes, puzzles and much more…

Housing

A fixed number of homes can be bought in the world, home owners can customize the home’s interior, invite other players, organize public events and even stake them to earn rewards!
